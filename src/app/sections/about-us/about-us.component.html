About Us

Ramya Traders is a trusted name in agri-based trading with over 25 years of retail experience, deeply rooted in the values of integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Founded from a successful family-run retail business specializing in bananas, fruits, and groceries, our legacy is built on direct relationships with farmers and communities across Tamil Nadu and South India.

Now, we are proud to take the next big leap — expanding into the global export market. With the same values that shaped our local success, Ramya Traders now aims to connect the world with India’s finest agricultural and food products.

We are committed to delivering 100% quality-assured goods by sourcing directly from farmers, ensuring freshness, hygiene, and international grading standards. With deep roots in agricultural sourcing and a hands-on understanding of farm-to-market dynamics, Ramya Traders has evolved into a merchant exporter focused on quality, consistency, and global reliability.
